About Us

I got my start in the supplemental heating business back in 1974. I was 10 years old! My dad bought a full tank of home heating oil and was angered by having to pay .70/ per gallon of heating oil. My, times have changed! My father installed a Glacier Bay coal stove in our fireplace, and it produced endless glorious heat. The kind of heat that made the paint soft on our wooden mantle and the casting of the stove glow red.
